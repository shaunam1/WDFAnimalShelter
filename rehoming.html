The Adoption Process

  1. Application: Fill out the 'Adoption Application Form'
  2. Acceptace: We will email you in 5-7 business days to let you know if you have made it to the next stage of the adoption process
  3. Home Visit: We will assess what kind of pets might best suit your living situation
  4. Pet Matchmaking: We choose some animals that we think you will love
  5. Shelter Visit: You visit the shelter to meet some of the animals we picked out for you
  6. Adoption: You take home your new best friend!
